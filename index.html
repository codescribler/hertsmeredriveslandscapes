Services

Everything your outdoor space needs — one team.

01 · Resin Bound

Resin Bound Driveways

Modern, smooth, permeable finish that lasts 15–20 years. Choose from a range of stone colours and blends. Installed in 1–3 days once the base is prepared, with UV-stable resin that keeps its colour. Every job is quoted to the exact square metre.

02 · Block Paving

Block Paving Driveways

The classic choice for a reason: huge variety of patterns, shapes and colours, strong enough for heavy vehicles, low-maintenance, and attractive for decades when laid by professionals. Built to last, easy to clean, boosts kerb appeal the moment it's down.

03 · Tarmac · From £40/m²

Tarmac Driveways

The most cost-effective surface per square metre — typically £40–£60/m² — and the quickest to install (1–2 days). Ideal for larger drives and practical homeowners who want a hard-wearing, no-nonsense finish that lasts 15–20 years.

04 · Patios & Paths

Patios, Paths & Steps

Block-paved patios, natural stone patios, imprinted concrete, crazy paving, and matching paths and steps. A properly installed patio turns a tired garden into somewhere you actually want to spend time — BBQs, coffee in the morning, kids kicking a ball about.

Answers

Your questions, answered honestly.

Do you ask for a deposit?

Never. You pay when the job's complete and you're 100% happy with it. It's the single thing that's kept us in business for 15 years.

How long does a typical driveway take?

A standard resin or block-paved driveway takes 7–10 days from start to handover. Tarmac is usually 1–2 days. We tell you the exact timeline at quote stage so you can plan around it.

How much does it cost?

It depends on the size, the surface you choose, and the state of what's currently there. Tarmac typically starts around £40–£60 per square metre. Resin and block paving are more. We give you a fixed written quote after a free on-site visit — no obligation, no pressure.

Are you insured?

Yes — fully insured, and our insurance is independently verified on our TrustATrader profile (verified October 2025). Member TT19764.

Do you work outside Hertfordshire?

Our sweet spot is Welwyn Garden City, Hatfield, St Albans, Potters Bar, Barnet and the surrounding Herts villages. If you're slightly further out, get in touch — we'll let you know honestly whether we can help or not.

What happens if something needs fixing after the job's done?

You ring John directly on the same number you rang for the quote. That's how it's worked for 15 years and that's how it'll work for the next 15. No call centres. No "support tickets". Just John.

Can I see examples of your work?

Yes — our gallery has 26+ finished projects across Hertfordshire, and you can read all 51 verified reviews on our TrustATrader profile. Every photo is real work, no stock imagery.
